I am a 5th-year Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering with a concentration in Robotics & Autonomous Systems at the University of Central Florida. My research focuses on developing probabilistic machine learning methods for learning-based state estimation and control of uncertain dynamical systems.

My work integrates neural processes, uncertainty quantification, multi-fidelity learning, reinforcement learning, and physics-informed machine learning for robotics, aerospace autonomy, and distributed multi-agent systems. I have conducted research through NASA Goddard Space Flight Center, the Air Force Research Laboratory, MIT, and Stanford University.

Expected Graduation: Summer 2027.
