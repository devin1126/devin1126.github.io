## Welcome

Thank you for visiting my personal research website repository.

This website serves as a central hub for my research, publications, conference presentations, technical projects, and professional experiences in robotics, machine learning, aerospace autonomy, and intelligent autonomous systems.

My research focuses on the development of learning-based state estimation and control algorithms for uncertain dynamical systems, with applications spanning autonomous vehicles, robotics, aerospace systems, and distributed multi-agent autonomy.

## Current Research Areas

### Physics-Informed Neural Processes

Development of probabilistic neural process architectures that integrate physical models directly into the learning process to improve estimation accuracy, robustness, generalization, and uncertainty calibration.

### Learning-Based Autonomous Control

Design of neural process-based controllers for autonomous navigation, obstacle avoidance, and high-speed autonomous racing.

### Multi-Agent State Estimation

Development of distributed learning architectures for cooperative state estimation under communication constraints and dynamically changing network topologies.

### Reinforcement Learning for Aerospace Guidance

Investigation of reinforcement learning-based guidance and planning systems for autonomous spacecraft operations and asteroid reconnaissance missions.
